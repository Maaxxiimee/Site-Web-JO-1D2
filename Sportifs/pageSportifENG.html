Felix Le Brun

Biography

Felix Le Brun is a young French table tennis athlete who distinguished himself in the table tennis world with his incredible skills at the Paris 2024 Olympic Games. A right-handed attacker playing in a featherweight grip, he has a style based on speed and variations of serve. Félix Lebrun has won numerous titles in youth categories, notably becoming French champion in the minime, cadet and junior categories. Currently 5 in the world rankings, he's one of the greats at just 18.

French table tennis player

The story of a Champion

Felix Le Brun, born on September 12, 2006 in Montpellier, is a promising young French table tennis talent. Born into a family of table tennis players, he is coached by his father Stéphane Lebrun and plays alongside his brother Alexis, also a top-level player. He quickly made a name for himself on the international scene, turning in impressive performances against experienced opponents, including world-class players. Considered one of table tennis's great hopefuls, he and his brother have helped put France at the forefront of the sport.

Felix Le Brun marked 2023 with major achievements, including a gold medal in singles at the European Games and victories on the world circuit such as at the WTT Star Contender Goa. He reached 4ᵉ world ranking in 2024 thanks to his offensive style and impressive victories against legendary players like Ma Long. At 18, he is one of the best table tennis players on the planet and a key figure in French table tennis
